The Neosantara Customer Chat Support widget is a powerful, customizable, and easy-to-embed tool that allows you to provide AI-powered customer support, sales assistance, or information services directly on your website. It supports modular configuration, document uploads, and a private Knowledge Base (RAG).

## Creating a Support Widget

Follow these steps to create and configure your Customer Chat Support widget:

1.  **Access the Dashboard**: Log in to the [Neosantara Dashboard](https://app.neosantara.xyz/dashboard).
2.  **Navigate to Integration**: Click on **Integration** in the sidebar, then select **Chat Widget**.
3.  **Create New Widget**: Click the **"Create Widget"** button to open the configurator.
4.  **Configure Settings**:
    *   **General**: Set your widget's name, choose an AI model, and list your **Allowed Domains** (e.g., `example.com`). You can use a **Template** (Customer Service, Sales, etc.) to quickly pre-fill the **System Prompt**.
    *   **Appearance**: Customize colors, choose a layout (`modern-round` or `classic-sharp`), and set the widget's placement on your screen.
    *   **Features**: Enable **File Uploads** or set a **Proactive Delay** to greet users automatically.
    *   **Knowledge**: Once you've saved your widget for the first time, you can upload PDFs or text files to the **Knowledge Base** to give your AI specific context about your business.
5.  **Save and Embed**: Click **Create** (or **Save**). Once saved, click the **"Get Code"** button on your widget's card to copy the embed snippet.

## Quick Start

To get started, you just need to embed a single `<script>` tag into your website's HTML, typically before the closing `</body>` tag.

```html
<!-- Neosantara Customer Chat Support -->
<script
  src="https://api.neosantara.xyz/widget.js"
  data-widget-id="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  async
></script>
```

Replace `YOUR_WIDGET_ID` with the ID provided in your [Neosantara Dashboard](https://app.neosantara.xyz/dashboard/integration/widget).

## Configuration

You can customize almost every aspect of the widget through the Neosantara Dashboard. Below are the available configuration categories:

### Appearance
Customize how the widget looks to match your brand's identity.

| Setting | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Theme Color** | The primary color used for buttons, headers, and accents. |
| **Logo URL** | Display your brand logo at the top of the chat window. |
| **Layout Style** | Choose between `modern-round`, `classic-square`, or `compact`. |
| **Launcher Style** | Choose how the widget button looks (`icon` or `text`). |
| **Placement** | Position the widget at `bottom-right` (default) or `bottom-left`. |
| **Offsets** | Fine-tune the position using `Offset X` and `Offset Y` (in pixels). |

### Behavior
Control how and where the widget interacts with your users.

| Setting | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Message** | The first message sent by the AI when a user opens the widget. |
| **Proactive Delay** | Automatically show a greeting bubble after a set delay (in milliseconds). |
| **Allowed Domains** | A whitelist of domains where the widget is permitted to load. |
| **Suppressed URLs** | Regex patterns for URLs where the widget should be hidden (e.g., `/checkout/*`). |

### AI & Knowledge Base
Configure the "brain" of your AI agent.

- **Model Selection**: Choose from various models like `Nusantara Base` for speed or `Archipelago 70B` for high-reasoning tasks.
- **System Prompt**: Define the personality, tone, and constraints of your AI agent.
- **Knowledge Base (RAG)**: Upload PDF, TXT, or DOCX files to give your AI specific knowledge about your business. Matches are automatically retrieved and injected into the AI's context.

### Advanced Features
- **File Uploads**: Allow users to upload documents or images directly in the chat. You can set an `Upload Size Limit` (default 25MB).
- **Remove Watermark**: Pro and Business tier users can remove the "Powered by Neosantara" branding.

## Technical Details

### Shadow DOM Isolation
The widget uses **Shadow DOM** to ensure that its styles do not conflict with your website's existing CSS. It lives under an element with the ID `#nusa-widget-root`.

### Pay-As-You-Go Billing
Usage for the widget (Chat tokens and Knowledge Base embeddings) is billed directly to your project's **Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG)** balance.
- **Chat**: Billed based on the tokens used in the conversation.
- **Knowledge Base**: A one-time fee is charged for embedding documents when they are uploaded.

## Troubleshooting

### Widget not appearing?
1. Check if the `data-widget-id` is correct.
2. Verify that your current domain is in the **Allowed Domains** list.
3. Check the browser console for any "Neosantara: data-widget-id is missing" or "Config not found" errors.
4. Ensure you haven't suppressed the current URL in the configuration.

### Uploads failing?
- Ensure `Allow Uploads` is enabled in your configuration.
- Check if your subscription tier supports file uploads.
- Verify the file size is within the configured limit.
